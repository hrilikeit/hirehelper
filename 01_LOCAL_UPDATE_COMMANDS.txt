STEP 1 - DOWNLOAD AND UNZIP THE PATCH

If the zip is in your Downloads folder, run:

cd ~/Downloads
rm -rf hirehelper-client-offer-billing-update-temp
unzip hirehelper-client-offer-billing-update.zip -d hirehelper-client-offer-billing-update-temp


STEP 2 - COPY THE PATCH INTO YOUR LOCAL LARAVEL PROJECT

Replace /FULL/PATH/TO/YOUR/LOCAL/LARAVEL/PROJECT with your real local project path, then run:

cd /FULL/PATH/TO/YOUR/LOCAL/LARAVEL/PROJECT
rsync -av ~/Downloads/hirehelper-client-offer-billing-update-temp/hirehelper-client-offer-billing-update/ ./


STEP 3 - RUN LARAVEL UPDATE COMMANDS LOCALLY

composer dump-autoload
php artisan optimize:clear
php artisan migrate
php artisan serve
